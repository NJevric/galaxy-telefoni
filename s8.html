Galaxy S8

Samsung Galaxy S8 The phone comes with a 5.80-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1440x2560 pixels.

Samsung Galaxy S8 is powered by a 2.35GHz octa-core Snapdragon 835 processor. It comes with 4GB of RAM.

The Samsung Galaxy S8 runs Android 7.0 and is powered by a 4,000mAh non-removable battery.

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Samsung Galaxy S8 on the rear packs 12-megapixel camera. It sports a 8-megapixel camera on the front for selfies.

Samsung Galaxy S8 based on Android 7.0 and packs 64GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card (up to 256GB). The Samsung Galaxy S8 Active is a single SIM (GSM + CDMA) smartphone that accepts a Nano-SIM card.

The Samsung Galaxy S8 measures 151.80 x 74.90 x 9.90mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 208.00 grams. It was launched in Meteor Grey and Titanium Gold colours.
