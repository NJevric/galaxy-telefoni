Galaxy S1

Samsung Galaxy S smartphone was launched in June 2010. The phone comes with a 4.00-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 480x800 pixels.

Samsung Galaxy S is powered by a 1GHz processor. It comes with 512MB of RAM.

The Samsung Galaxy S runs Android 2.1 and is powered by a 1,500mAh removable battery.

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Samsung Galaxy S on the rear packs 5-megapixel camera. It sports a 0.3-megapixel camera on the front for selfies.

Samsung Galaxy S based on Android 2.1 and packs 16GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card (up to 32GB). The Samsung Galaxy S is a single SIM (GSM) smartphone that accepts a Regular-SIM card.

Connectivity options on the Samsung Galaxy S include Wi-Fi, GPS, Bluetooth v3.00, FM radio, and 3G. Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, proximity sensor, and compass/ magnetometer.

The Samsung Galaxy S measures 122.40 x 64.20 x 9.90mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 118.00 grams. It was launched in BLACK and WHITE colours.
